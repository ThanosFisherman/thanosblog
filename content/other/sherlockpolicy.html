WiFi Sherlock requires 3 critical permissions in order to be functional. Identity permission enables WiFi Sherlock to fetch your emails from your accounts list and use them when possible to autocomplete the username/email textbox during the login process. Storage permission is used to store venues in phone's memory for faster retrieval and better user experience. Location permission makes WiFi Sherlock location-aware so that it can search and find venues around you.
